My name is Zsombor Rajki, a second-year Computer Science student at Eindhoven University of Technology. I'm originally from Hungary and now based in the Netherlands. I'm passionate about problem-solving, mathematics, and creating technology that combines functionality with thoughtful design. My journey into computer science began with simple curiosity, which grew into a strong drive to learn, build, and challenge myself. I see computer science as a powerful tool for bringing ideas to life. It blends logic, creativity, and persistence to create meaningful and intuitive solutions. Alongside my studies, I work on real-world projects, including developing computer vision systems for a beach-cleaning robot, as well as several personal side projects. In my free time, I find inspiration in movies, hiking and nature. I'm always aiming to stay curious and keep growing.
